About Travel to Greece

Since 1987, Travel to Greece has been dedicated to offering travelers authentic, immersive, and unforgettable experiences across the stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Greece. As a family-owned business, we have always believed that travel is not just about seeing new places, but about creating meaningful connections with people, cultures, and nature.

Our Story

Founded by George and Maria Papadopoulos, Travel to Greece began with a simple vision: to share the beauty and spirit of Greece with the world. Starting with small, personalized tours to George’s home island of Karpathos, the company has grown into a trusted name in Greek travel, known for its attention to detail, passionate guides, and commitment to sustainable tourism. Today, their children, Nikos and Eleni, continue the family tradition, ensuring that each traveler experiences Greece like a local.

Our Team

What sets us apart is our people. Our team is made up of passionate travel experts, each with deep roots in different parts of Greece. They bring their personal experiences, cultural insights, and local connections to every trip, making your journey with us truly unique. From guides who know the hidden gems of every island to travel planners who design the perfect itinerary, every member of our team is dedicated to making your trip exceptional.

Meet Our Team:

Nikos Papadopoulos – Head of Operations

Nikos grew up exploring the hills and beaches of Karpathos, and his love for Greece’s natural beauty shines through in every trip he plans. With over 20 years of experience, he ensures every journey runs smoothly from start to finish.

Eleni Papadopoulos – Customer Experience Manager

Eleni’s passion is creating personalized, authentic experiences for our clients. Her deep knowledge of Greek culture and her attention to detail mean that no two trips are ever the same. Eleni works closely with travelers to design unique, tailor-made itineraries.

Stavros Dimitriou – Lead Tour Guide

Stavros is an adventurer at heart, with a deep love for Greece’s history and mythology. His enthusiasm and storytelling bring the ancient ruins and landscapes of Greece to life for our guests. Whether it’s a hike through the mountains or a tour of an ancient site, Stavros ensures every experience is engaging and educational.

Maria Konstantinou – Culinary Tour Expert

A native of Crete, Maria is passionate about sharing Greece’s rich culinary traditions with travelers. She curates food tours that introduce guests to local ingredients, traditional recipes, and the stories behind every dish.

Why Choose Us?

Experience – With over 35 years of expertise, we know Greece inside and out, offering unparalleled access to the country’s hidden treasures.

Personalized Service – No two trips are alike. We design each itinerary based on your interests, whether it’s history, adventure, gastronomy, or relaxation.

Local Connections – Our deep ties to local communities allow us to offer you experiences you won’t find in any guidebook, from private tours of family-owned vineyards to exclusive cultural festivals.

Sustainability – We are committed to preserving the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Greece. Our tours support local businesses, and we work to minimize the environmental impact of our travels.
